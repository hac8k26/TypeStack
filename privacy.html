Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Short version: TypeStack does not collect, store, or sell any personal data. We have no accounts, no tracking, and no analytics. Fonts and icons are loaded from third-party CDNs (Google Fonts, jsDelivr) which have their own privacy policies.

1 Information We Collect

TypeStack does not collect any personal information. There are no user accounts, no login forms, no cookies set by us, and no analytics scripts. We do not track your browsing behavior, search queries, or font selections.

2 Third-Party Services

TypeStack loads resources from the following third-party services. When your browser fetches these resources, those services may log your IP address and request metadata according to their own privacy policies.

Google Fonts Serves font files. Google may collect usage data. See Google's Privacy Policy.
jsDelivr CDN Serves the Lucide icon library. See jsDelivr's Privacy Policy.

3 Cookies

TypeStack does not set any cookies. Third-party services listed above may set their own cookies subject to their respective policies.

4 Local Storage

TypeStack does not use localStorage, sessionStorage, or IndexedDB to store any data about you.

5 Font Downloads

When you use the download feature, your browser directly fetches font files from Google Fonts servers. This request is made from your browser to Google — TypeStack does not proxy or log these requests.

6 Children's Privacy

TypeStack does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since we collect no personal data from anyone, this applies equally to all users.

7 Changes to This Policy

If we ever change this policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Since we collect no data, any changes would only reflect updates to third-party services or legal requirements.

8 Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Reach out via the TypeStack project page.
